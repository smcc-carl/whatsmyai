Dora.run

Dora.run is a platform designed to help engineering teams measure and improve their software delivery performance using the DevOps Research and Assessment (DORA) framework. It provides tools to track key metrics like deployment frequency, lead time, change failure rate, and mean time to recovery, offering actionable insights to optimize workflows and enhance efficiency. With intuitive dashboards and seamless integration into existing CI/CD pipelines, Dora.run empowers teams to align with DevOps best practices and drive continuous improvement in their development processes.
